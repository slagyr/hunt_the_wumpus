A player can rest for his turn.  This is a valid command that does not move the player.

!include DonutMap

!|script|
|put|player|in cavern|1|
|enter command|Rest|
|check|cavern|1|has|player|true|
|check|message|I don't know how to Rest.|was printed|false|

!|script|
|enter command|rest|
|check|cavern|1|has|player|true|
